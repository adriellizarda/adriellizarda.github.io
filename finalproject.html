CSC 106 FINAL PROJECT


This is our final project for CSC: 106 at URI.

Burger Shot is a restaurant simulation game: you play as an overworked frycook serving artery clogging burgers to hungry customers.

A customer will approach from the left side of the screen with a randomized order. To satisfy them, build their burger by clicking on the right ingredients and serve it to them by clicking on the plate within a fifteen second timer (which will shorten to six seconds as the game progresses).

To cook a burger, click on the patty–this will place meat onto the stove and start a visible timer. To put it in your burger, you need to click on it again when the timer turns green. If it’s yellow, it’ll be raw, and if it’s red, it’ll be burnt. You can cook multiple burgers at once if you’d like.

If the customer is satisfied, they’ll pay you and leave. If the customer isn’t satisfied (you don’t make the time limit, serve the wrong order, or burn their patty) they’ll leave upset. To win, you need to make 200$ before all customers pass.

Good luck and have fun!
